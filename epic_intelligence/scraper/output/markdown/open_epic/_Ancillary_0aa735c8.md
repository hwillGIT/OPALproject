# 
 Ancillary Systems

 

 

 
 Exchange information about lab results, pharmacy, and other ancillary clinical systems

 

 
 

 
 

 
 
 

 

 Ancillary systems, such as lab, pharmacy, cardiology, radiology, and other systems, typically sit apart from the core clinical functions of an electronic
 health record. Epic provides native ancillary systems that might need to interoperate with other healthcare systems, or you can use a third party that needs
 to integrate with your Epic EHR.
