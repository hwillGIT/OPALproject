# 
 Clinical Information

 

 

 
 Sending and receiving clinical data.

 

 
 

 
 

 
 
 

 
Clinical data in the Epic EHR takes many forms, depending on when it is used, who documents it, and who needs to view it. We have many methods by which you
 can send and receive clinical data from the EHR, some of which are very specific and some of which are more general.
