# 
 Developer Support
 

 

 

 Epic is here to provide tools and support for the adoption and deployment of standards-based
 data exchange.
 

 

 

 

 
## 
 open.epic
 

 

 Epic has released hundreds of industry-standard and public APIs, open to everyone, and
 available free to app developers. We recommend beginning with these standards as the
 foundation for your design solution. Building on that, Epic offers additional self-service resources,
 including the
 [Design Overview](/DesignOverview),
 [App Developer Guidelines](https://fhir.epic.com/Documentation?docId=developerguidelines), and
 [Data Sharing Playbooks](/Playbooks).
 We've also seen how essential customer engagement and feedback are to success, so we've
 compiled
 [recommended practices](https://fhir.epic.com/Documentation?docId=eofcollaborate)
 for working with healthcare customers based on our experiences.
 

 

 For procedural questions about building and distributing apps or issues with accounts or
 access,
 [contact our open.epic team](mailto:open@epic.com).
 

 

 For questions about Epic's support for data sharing technologies or if you haven't been able
 to determine an API that meets your data exchange requirements, complete the
 [Technology Guidance](/Contact#DataSharingGuidance) form.
 

 

 If you run into technical issues, we recommend first referencing our
 [Web App and Web Service Troubleshooting guide](https://fhir.epic.com/Documentation?docId=troubleshooting_eof)
 for possible solutions. If you're working with an Epic customer, you
 may also find a resolution by requesting support from their IT team.
 

 

 If you are looking for more help from Epic tailored to your efforts to exchange data, you
 may be interested in our
 Vendor Services
 program.
 

 

 

 

 

 

 
## 
 Vendor Services
 

 

 Vendor Services
 is an optional support offering from Epic, available to any
 organization that meets the basic enrollment criteria.
 Vendor Services
 builds on everything
 available on open.epic by offering additional technologies and services to build and enable your
 app's connection to Epic software. Once enrolled, you can contact a pool of Epic TS representatives
 for Q&A-style support for services such as integration design feedback, direct troubleshooting
 assistance, install support, and more. Enrolled vendors also can access an expanded API
 specifications catalog to enable their data exchange where industry-standard APIs may not yet exist
 or fully meet the needs of a specific use case.
 

 

 To learn more or enroll, visit the
 [Vendor Services website](https://vendorservices.epic.com)
 or [contact our Vendor Services team](mailto:VendorServices@epic.com)
