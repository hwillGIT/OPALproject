# 
 Financial

 

 

 
 Exchange information about money that's due and money that's paid.

 

 
 

 
 

 
 
 

 

 Epic provides software for enterprise registration, hospital billing, professional billing, claims, and a managed care suite. These interfaces cover a wide
 range of functionality broadly related to how organizations charge for the care they provide and how those charges are paid for.
