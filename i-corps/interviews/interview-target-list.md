### H7 — What does the buying process actually look like?

- "Walk me through the last clinical tool your hospital adopted. How long did it take from first conversation to go-live?"
- "Who are the key decision-makers when a new nursing workflow tool is being evaluated?"
- "What kills most new tool proposals before they get approved?"
- "What's the difference between a tool that gets piloted and a tool that gets adopted system-wide?"

### H8 — Will hospitals pay, and where does budget come from?

- "Where does budget for nursing workflow tools come from — nursing, IT, innovation, quality, somewhere else?"
- "What metrics would you need to see to approve a new clinical tool?"
- "Is there a difference between what gets funded as a pilot versus what gets funded for full deployment?"
- "How do you think about ROI for tools that improve nursing workflow?"

### H9 — Is this a funded priority for the economic buyer?

- "Do you see information access and communication as a priority problem for your nursing staff?"
- "Where does this rank against your other priorities — staffing, retention, EHR optimization, patient experience?"
- "If someone came to you with a solution to this problem, would you have budget to explore it?"
- "Have you actively looked for a solution to this, or is it something you've accepted as how hospitals work?"

### H10 — Is information siloed, and would hospitals support consolidating it?

- "How many different systems does a nurse need to access during a shift?"
- "Where do hospital protocols, policies, and clinical guidelines actually live?"
- "Has anyone attempted to unify clinical content into a single access point?"
- "What would need to be true for someone to successfully unify that information?"

### H11 — Is EHR integration accessible to an early-stage company?

- "What does Epic integration look like for a new clinical tool?"
- "Is there an API layer or FHIR interface that third-party tools can use?"
- "How does your hospital handle clinical decision support today?"
- "What's the realistic path for a startup to integrate with your EHR?"

### H12 — Can hospital infrastructure support a new clinical device?

- "Could your hospital's network infrastructure support a new connected device?"
- "How does your hospital manage connected devices on the clinical network?"
- "Walk me through how a new connected device gets approved for your hospital network."
- "What are the security and compliance requirements a new clinical tool must meet?"

## End-of-Interview Referral Ask

"This has been incredibly helpful. I'm also trying to understand how this looks from the leadership and technology side — people like CNOs, innovation officers, or informatics leads. Is there anyone in your network who you think would be open to a similar 20-minute conversation? Even just a name and I can reach out on my own."

If they offer to make an intro: "That would be amazing. A quick email or LinkedIn message from you would go a long way."

If they hesitate: "No pressure at all. Even knowing who the right person to talk to would be helpful."
