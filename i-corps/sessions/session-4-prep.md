*This is NOT another weekly update. It's the story of your customer discovery journey.*

- Highlight your "a-ha!" moment(s)

- End with Next Steps decision

**Key rule:** Your slide deck supports your storytelling. Do NOT just narrate your slides.

**Text:** Brief and legible — large enough to read.
